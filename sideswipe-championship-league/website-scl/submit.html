Submit your match result screenshots for verification by league admins.

What is Match Submit?

The Match Submit Portal allows Players and Team Leaders to upload screenshots of their match results for official verification by league admins.

How it works

  • Upload up to 5 game screenshots (Best of 5 matches)
  • Admins review and verify the results
  • Scores are updated in the official standings

Who can submit?

Only registered Players or Team Leaders can submit match results. If you're a viewer, you can still follow the league on our other pages!
